Help A Brother Out

Rowing is a very rewarding yet expensive sport. Regatta fees, travel expenses, equipment maintenance are only some of the costs to compete against the biggest teams in the south. In order to keep member fees as low as possible, we need people like you to help us. If you are interest in donating to the LSU Rowing Club, fill out this form and our sponsorship coordinator will get back to you as soon as possible.

Thank you so much for your support and REAUX TIGERS!

If you are interested in sponsoring, donating, or fundraising, please contact our fundraising coordinator Aine O'nuain at aonia2@lsu.edu
