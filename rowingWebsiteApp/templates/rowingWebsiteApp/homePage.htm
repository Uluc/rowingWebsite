Who Are We?

Each person who walks on our dock has a different story of how they got there but we all have one thing in common, dedication. Most of us didn't know the first thing about rowing when we started but we have grown together as a crew and as a team to compete in regattas across the south. LSU Rowing aims to employ our mission statement with every practice and regatta we attend.
